Dojoclose

Similar to Duolingo, this is the project I learned angular on. It is an app to teach Danish to native English speakers. The app contains four lessons inside unit one, with placeholder images for the remaining units. The lessons consist of eight different card types. I built a custom directive for each of those card types. As you cycle through my lesson array stored in Mongodb, the custom directives hide/show themselves based on the current slide.

About Me


Joshua LeDuc

My passions lie within technology, culture, and education.

I have been building PCs and tinkering around with gadgets for as long as I can remember. My interests in hardware repair and coding have evolved naturally as I have learned that my biggest childhood hobby can be used to solve real world problems.

My love of culture first started when I moved from the Mid-West of the United States to the cosmopolitan Montréal, Québec. The wonderful mash up of American and European culture opened my eyes to the many angles one can use to look at the same situation.

Finally, I feel that education can solve problems and empower people over the long term. If used correctly, technology can revolutionize and improve education the same way it has for many other industries in our recent history.
